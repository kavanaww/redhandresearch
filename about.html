Why We Exist

We exist because reproducible science requires reliable materials. Full stop.

Our Process

What Gold Standard Actually Means

We use the phrase deliberately — because we hold ourselves to what it actually requires, not what it sounds like marketing-wise.

01

Identity Verification

Every compound is confirmed by mass spectrometry before it leaves the lab. We verify what it is, not just what we think it is.

02

HPLC Purity Analysis

Purity is quantified by high-performance liquid chromatography — the analytical standard for peptide characterization — per lot.

03

Endotoxin Testing

Bacterial endotoxin levels are reported per lot using LAL methodology to protect the integrity of cell-based research assays.

04

Dual Sterility Labs

Microbial contamination testing is performed by two independent accredited laboratories per lot — not one.

05

Elemental Screening

Heavy metal and elemental impurity screening is included per lot to eliminate trace contaminants that can confound analytical outcomes.

06

Lot-Specific CoA

Every product ships with a downloadable Certificate of Analysis tied to its exact lot — not a generic certificate from a different batch.
