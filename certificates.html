Our Standards

What Every Lot Is Tested For

Independent third-party testing is conducted on every lot before it ships. Here's what's verified on every compound we carry.

Identity & Purity

HPLC and mass spectrometry confirm compound identity and purity to ≥99% on every lot. No lot ships without passing this threshold.

≥99% Purity

Microbial Sterility

Two independent sterility labs test each lot for microbial contamination. Dual-lab verification eliminates single-source error.

2× Independent Labs

Bacterial Endotoxins

LAL (Limulus Amebocyte Lysate) testing is performed per lot to detect bacterial endotoxins that can interfere with in-vitro research outcomes.

Per Lot

Elemental Impurities

Heavy metal and trace element screening per ICH Q3D guidelines. Ensures no elemental contamination that could confound analytical results.

ICH Q3D

Lot Consistency

Multi-point analytical verification ensures consistency across vials within a lot and across sequential lots of the same compound.

Full Traceability
