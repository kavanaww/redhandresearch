BPC-157

Body Protection Compound-157 • Pentadecapeptide

BPC-157 is a synthetic pentadecapeptide — a chain of 15 amino acids — derived from a protective protein found naturally in gastric juice. It is among the most widely studied peptides in preclinical research, with a substantial body of literature examining its effects on tissue repair, angiogenesis, and systemic recovery across multiple organ systems.

Research has examined BPC-157 across a broad range of tissue types, including musculoskeletal, gastrointestinal, tendon, ligament, bone, and neural tissue. Studies suggest it may upregulate growth hormone receptor expression and modulate nitric oxide synthesis, pathways linked to accelerated healing responses in preclinical models.

All Red Hand Research BPC-157 is supplied as a lyophilized powder in sterile glass vials, verified by independent third-party HPLC and mass spectrometry analysis to ≥99% purity.
